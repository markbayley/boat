The Great Wave

This website is based on the art of the Japanese artist Hokusai. The Great Wave off Kanagawa is a woodblock print by the Japanese ukiyo-e artist Hokusai. It was published sometime between 1829 and 1833 in the late Edo period as the first print in Hokusai's series "Thirty-Six Views of Mount Fuji".
